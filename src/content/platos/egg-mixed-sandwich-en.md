## 🍳 Ham, Cheese & Egg Sandwich

### Short Description

An upgraded and nutritious version of the classic "mixto" (ham and cheese) sandwich. It combines traditional York ham and cheese with the addition of an egg, complemented by the freshness of tomato, lettuce, and creamy avocado slices.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Sliced bread
* York ham
* Cheese
* Fried or grilled egg
* Sliced tomato
* Fresh lettuce
* Sliced avocado
