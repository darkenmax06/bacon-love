## 🐙 Galician-Style Octopus (Pulpo á feira)

### Short Description

A traditional Galician dish. Boiled octopus sliced and served over a bed of boiled potatoes, seasoned with extra virgin olive oil, coarse sea salt, and paprika (usually a blend of sweet and spicy).

### 📝 Ingredients

* Boiled octopus
* Boiled potatoes
* Extra virgin olive oil
* Coarse sea salt
* Paprika (sweet and/or spicy)
