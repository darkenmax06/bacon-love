## 🍳 Chile Fríe Egg

### Short Description

A comforting dish that combines the intensity of chili con carne with the creamy texture of a fried egg. Ideal for a savory and hearty bite.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Chili base (with meat or stew-style)
* Fried or grilled egg
* (Optional: often served with bread or tortillas, though not specified)
