## 🤯 Big Hot Dog

### Short Description

The ultimate hot dog, a tower of flavors and textures with an epic combination of ingredients. Atop a juicy sausage, you'll find bacon bits, melted cheddar cheese, the crunch of Doritos and crispy onions, the spicy kick of jalapeños and chilies, and the tangy-sweet flavor of pickles. Finished with a blend of mayonnaise, ketchup, and BBQ sauce.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Hot dog bun
* Sausage
* Bacon ❤
* Cheddar cheese sauce
* Mayonnaise
* Ketchup
* BBQ sauce
* Doritos (crushed)
* Crispy onion
* Chilies
* Jalapeños
* Sliced pickles
* Corn
