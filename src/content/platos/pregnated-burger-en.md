## 🤰 Preñada (Stuffed)

### Short Description

A very hearty burger with a flavorful surprise inside. The **200g dry-aged beef** patty is accompanied by Edam cheese and bacon. Its main highlight is the bun or stuffing containing a powerful blend of **Mozzarella, cream cheese, Cheddar, and ham**. Fresh vegetables and bacon sauce complete the experience.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Base: Mozzarella, cream cheese, Cheddar, and ham
* Dry-aged beef (200g)
* Edam cheese
* Bacon ❤❤
* Fresh lettuce
* Sliced tomato
* Bacon sauce
