## 🍚 Black Rice

### Short Description
Rice cooked with a variety of **seafood** and natural squid or cuttlefish ink, which gives it its characteristic black color and intense flavor.

### 📝 Ingredients
* Rice
* Squid/cuttlefish ink
* Seafood
* Fish and seafood stock
