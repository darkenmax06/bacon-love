## 🐟 Sea Bream Stuffed with Paella

### Short Description

Whole deboned sea bream stuffed with a portion of flavorful **seafood paella**. A dish that fuses the taste of the sea with traditional rice craftsmanship.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Sea bream
* Seafood paella stuffing (rice, seafood, broth)
