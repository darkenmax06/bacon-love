## 👑 Classic

### Short Description

The essential burger elevated to the highest quality. A juicy **200g dry-aged beef** patty grilled to perfection, served on a tender **brioche bun** and topped with a layer of creamy melted Cheddar cheese. Simply seasoned with the foolproof combination of ketchup and mayonnaise.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Brioche bun
* Dry-aged beef (200g)
* Cheddar cheese
* Ketchup
* Mayonnaise
