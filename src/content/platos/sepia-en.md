## 🦑 Sautéed Cuttlefish in Black Butter

### Short Description

Cuttlefish strips sautéed in **black butter** (clarified butter cooked until the milk solids turn a dark amber color with a nutty flavor). It is served accompanied by a creamy **potato parmentier**.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Cuttlefish
* Black butter
* Potato parmentier
