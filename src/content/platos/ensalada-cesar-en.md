## 🥗 Caesar Salad

### Short Description
The classic and popular Caesar salad featuring mixed greens, pieces of **crispy fried chicken**, toasted croutons, Parmesan cheese, and the unmistakable **Caesar dressing**.

### 📝 Key Ingredients
* Mixed greens
* Crispy chicken, croutons
* Parmesan cheese
* Caesar dressing
