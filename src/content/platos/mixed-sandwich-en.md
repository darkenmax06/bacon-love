## 🥪 Ham and Cheese Sandwich (Sándwich Mixto)

### Short Description

The unbeatable classic, perfect for any time of day. Simple and delicious, featuring cooked ham and melted cheese between two slices of toasted bread.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Sliced bread
* Cooked ham (York ham)
* Cheese
