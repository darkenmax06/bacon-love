## 🍚 Iberian Rice

### Short Description
A hearty mountain-style rice dish featuring the intense flavor of **Iberian "secreto" pork** and a mix of fresh vegetables.

### 📝 Ingredients
* Rice
* Iberian "secreto" pork
* Vegetables (e.g., peppers, green beans)
* Meat broth
