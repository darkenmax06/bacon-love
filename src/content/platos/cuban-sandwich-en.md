## 🇨🇺 Cuban Sandwich (Bacon Love Style)

### Short Description

While it takes its name from the Cuban classic, this version is a generous stack of flavor and texture. Served on sliced bread, it features fresh tomato, lettuce, cheese, York ham, chicken, crispy bacon, and egg. All held together by a creamy Marie Rose sauce (pink sauce) that adds a distinctive touch.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Sliced bread
* Cheese
* York ham
* Egg
* Chicken (sliced or shredded)
* Bacon ❤❤
* Fresh lettuce
* Sliced tomato
* Marie Rose sauce (Pink sauce)
