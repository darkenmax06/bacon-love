## 🥩 Carbonara Beef Cachopo

### Short Description

A delicious and hearty twist on the Asturian classic. Two breaded beef fillets stuffed with a rich *carbonara* filling of ham, cheese, and bacon. Served with crispy French fries and traditional Padrón peppers.

### 📝 Ingredients

* Beef fillets (for cachopo)
* Carbonara filling (Ham, cheese, bacon❤❤)
* French fries
* Padrón peppers
