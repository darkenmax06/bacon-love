## 🍚 Arroz al Señoret (Peeled Seafood Rice)

### Short Description
Rice cooked in a flavorful fish stock with a selection of **seafood and fish**. It is characterized by having all ingredients peeled or cleaned, "ready for the little lord" (*señoret*), so you don't have to use your hands.

### 📝 Ingredients
* Rice
* Seafood (cleaned and peeled)
* Fish and seafood stock
