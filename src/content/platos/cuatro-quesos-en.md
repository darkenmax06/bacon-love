## 🍝 Four Cheese Pasta

### Short Description
Pasta coated in a rich and velvety sauce made from a combination of **four different cheeses** (typically Parmesan, Gorgonzola, Fontina, and Provolone, among others).

### 📝 Key Ingredients
* Pasta
* Four-cheese blend
